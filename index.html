Hi there. We're the Emerald's creative agency. We're a group of thinkers and makers who want to make Eugene a better place to call home by creating fun, engaging content that taps into exactly what it means to be a student on our beautiful campus.

What we've done.

We pride ourselves on tackling all kinds of creative business problems. Advertising isn't about making a series of print ads anymore; We've got to make content, and more than that, make content that matters. So far, our projects have included photography, videography, logo design, web development, social media campaigns, and a few more odds and ends. We already understand our core target audience—students—so the hard part is over. Making the actual execution is the fun bit.

UO Sorority Bid Day 2013

Fraternity and Sorority Life at the University of Oregon contacted us about capturing the events of Bid Day 2013, the day when girls are accepted into the houses they'll bond with for the next four years. These are pieces of the photo essay that we created in order to document the ensuing hullabaloo.

SexPositive Promo

The University of Oregon Health Center came to us wanting to promote their newly developed app, SexPositive. We created this spot in order to spark downloads within the student population and minimize the stigma around talking about sex education around campus.

Radical Something

Radical Something are half-rock, half-reggae, and half-something else entirely. The University of Oregon Cultural Forum wanted the concert (which happened directly beneath the Venture Dept. offices, in the EMU Amphitheater) to be documented for posterity, so we sent our multimedia team to work their magic.



Located in the top-most tower in the University of Oregon's Erb Memorial Union, The Venture Dept. is a team of highly creative students who are passionate about making work that matters.

There's a lot of shitty advertising in the world. College students are constantly bombarded with ads that insult their taste and intelligence, but it doesn't have to be that way. We can make fun, engaging content that taps into exactly what it means to be a media-savvy student on our beautiful campus. Let's improve that campus climate, one great video (or print ad, or website, or social campaign, or photo story) at a time.

Success happens when brands and people connect on a personal level, when relationships form from common values. We value creativity, strategic thinking, beautiful design, and celebratory high-fives.

Let's make sure we have a little bit more of all of those in Eugene.
