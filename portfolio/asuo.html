A NEW LOOK FOR THE CENTRAL STUDENT ORGANIZATION ON CAMPUS

CLIENT: The Associated Students of the University of Oregon

AMBITION: Create a visual identity that combines established values and color palettes with a timeless look that will resonate with students.

SERVICES: Design, video production.

The Associated Students of the University of Oregon, the student government on the UO campus that is responsible for more than $14 million dollars in student fees, came to The Venture Dept. in seek of a new visual identity. The organization’s mission is that “the ASUO strives to provide for the social, cultural, educational and physical development of its members, and for the advancement of their individual and collective interests both within the University and the community at-large,” so we knew that we’d have to make it relevant and beautiful. The new look needed to capture the mission of the organization and stay current for years to come.

The logo that Venture created centers around a webfoot and a familiar color scheme to represent the university, but muted colors and round lines offer a modern twist on a rigid identity. The new brand typography offers the same soft lines, but still captures the seriousness of a more traditional type. The type and mark are meant to remain together as often as possible, but individually they remain recognizable assets.

With this new look, we created both business cards and a video bug to help brand all video releases. These executions maintain the brand across many kinds of media, ensuring the ASUO is an easily recognizable face on campus to all students.
