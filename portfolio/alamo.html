LEVERAGING #GODUCKS AS A POSITIVE REPRESENTATION OF THE ENTIRE UNIVERSITY

CLIENT: University of Oregon Marketing and Brand Management

AMBITION: Capture the spirit of Oregon Football fans at the 2013 Alamo Bowl by demonstrating the University of Oregon experience that includes academic and athletic excellence, community service, and fun.

SERVICES: Video production

When the Ducks played in the Alamo Bowl in late December 2013, the University of Oregon wanted to capture the moment in a way that could be shared with fans that couldn’t make it to San Antonio. By documenting events surrounding the actual game, UO was able to celebrate both the athletic achievement of the event as well as the academic achievement of students and faculty.

The Venture Dept. chronicled UO’s collaborations with University of Texas and boosted enthusiasm for postseason play through a series of videos that the University of Oregon could easily share with its students, staff, faculty, and alumni. This represented the University of Oregon as a top public research university known for its academic and athletic excellence, supporting the overall goal of the institution through creative work that resonated with its fans.
