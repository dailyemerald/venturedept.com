PLAN, PREPARE, AND PRACTICE!

CLIENT: University of Oregon Enterprise Risk Services

AMBITION: Educate students about how to prepare for campus emergencies in a way that doesn’t make them fall asleep.

SERVICES: Writing, video production

Fires, earthquakes, and other emergencies are bound to happen on campuses across the country, but Enterprise Risk Services at the University of Oregon is the department in charge of making sure that everyone associated with the college knows how to respond when they do.

Getting messages about exit routes and emergency kits to students is difficult, because millennials are likely to skip a safety video in favor of the most recent viral video sensation. We knew that to get students interested in this important information, we’d need to create a video that felt like a fresh take on campus safety.

We created this video, which riffs on airline safety instructions, in order to present the information in a humorous way. The final product was delivered to Enterprise Risk Services and posted to the official University of Oregon YouTube channel, broadcasting our project to its 5,600 followers. Students were entertained and educated, and will be much more prepared for the next time a week-long ice storm hits Eugene.
